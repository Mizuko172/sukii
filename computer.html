Computer

Updated: 04/09/2024 byComputer Hope

A computer is a programmable device that stores,retrieves, and processes data. The term was orginally given to humans (human computers) who performed numerical caculations using mechanical caculators, such as the abacus and slide rule. The term was later given to mechanical devices as they began replacing human computers. Today's computers are electronic devices that accept data. (input), process that data, produce output , and store (storage) the results (IPOS).
